ABOUT US

ACCESSING EDUCATION, TRANSFORMING LIVES

Fund Frontier is a public-benefit organisation, launched in June 2017 in response to India's #FeesMustFall movement, with the belief that access to education should not be dependent on wealth. Our founding partner, Standard Bank, shares in this belief and without their financial support and guidance, Fund Frontier would not be in existence today.

We help make education more accessible by connecting tertiary students in need to donor communities looking to make a difference through a secure Crowdfunding platform where students actively fundraise for their studies and through partnership with forward-thinking Businesses , where we administer Skills Development Bursary program opportunities on their behalf. In addition, we intentionally educate and equip young people to thrive through holistic support focussed interventions, enabling financial literacy, job readiness and personal mastery.

Our team is a vibrant tapestry of youth, diversity, and an unwavering commitment to have a lasting impact by enabling everyday people and businesses to solve an everyday problem. In 2020, we expanded our impact with WaFunda , a Fund Frontier Social Enterprise which is 100% owned by the Fund Frontier Trust and is an Exempted Micro Enterprise (EME) as defined in the B-BBEE Act. WaFunda is fueled by a singular purpose: driving social change in harmony with Fund Frontier's vision.

Together, Fund Frontier and WaFunda are dedicated to empowering students, dismantling educational barriers, and fostering a more inclusive society.
Join us in transforming lives through education.

OUR MISSION

To connect communities in an innovative way to achieve debt-free education, as well as to educate and equip young people to thrive.

OUR VISION

A society in which all young people are able to fulfill their potential regardless of wealth and positively contribute toward a healthier society.
