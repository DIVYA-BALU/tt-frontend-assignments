Taking Exam on Desktop PC / Laptop

  1. Please ensure that you are taking the exam on Chrome Browser. Upgraded browser gives a smoother experience.
  2. At the start of the exam, the browser may show pop ups for allowing permissions. Always click on Allow.
  3. Make sure your integrated webcam or external webcam is functioning properly.
  4. Do not navigate to any other window during the exam.
  5. Ensure that you have a secondary backup device/internet connection which will be useful when the primary device/internet is not working.
  6. .Ensure that the laptop battery is charged to maximum extent possible by the start time of the exam. If you are using a desktop PC please see if you can arrange for an UPS connection to continue exam even during power cuts or at least have a mobile phone as a backup.
  7. Disable all notification sending applications like Anti Virus Apps,etc.
