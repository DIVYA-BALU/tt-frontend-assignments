About Doctor Appointment Booking


Welcome to the Doctor Appointment Booking application. This application allows users to conveniently book appointments with their preferred doctors online.


Features:

  • Easy appointment booking process
  • Selection of preferred doctor
  • Flexible date and time selection
  • Confirmation message after successful appointment booking

How to Use:

  1. Go to the Booking Page to book your appointment.
  2. Fill in your details and select your preferred doctor, date, and time.
  3. Click the "Book Appointment" button to confirm your booking.
  4. After successful booking, you will see a confirmation message.
